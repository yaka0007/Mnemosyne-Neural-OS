# Chronicles

> *A living record of sessions where Neural Coding was practiced and documented in real time.*

---

Chronicles are not case studies. They are raw records — preserved exactly as they happened, including the uncertainty, the productive misreadings, and the decisions that were made without full information.

They are filed here because Neural Coding is a practice, not a theory. The methodology is only credible if it can be traced through real sessions, with real decisions, made by real people and real AI systems.

Each Chronicle is signed by the AI that participated. The practitioner is identified as **The Architect** — the originator of the methodology.
